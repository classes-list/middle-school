👋 Welcome

Aloha families! Welcome to 3rd Grade A. This year we will read great books, write with confidence, become fluent in multiplication and division, explore the world through science and social studies, and grow in our Catholic faith together. This syllabus is a quarter-by-quarter map of the year ahead so that families know what to expect and how to support learning at home.

🎯 Grading & Assessment

Student progress is reported quarterly. Grades are based on daily work, formative and summative assessments, class participation, and project-based learning aligned with each subject's Essential Questions.

  • Daily Work & Practice — classwork, weekly practice
  • Assessments — quizzes, chapter/unit tests, benchmark reads
  • Projects — writing pieces, novels studies, science labs, social studies research
  • Participation & Effort — classroom discussions, prayer, service

🏫 Classroom Community & Expectations

Our classroom follows Mary, Star of the Sea School's values of respect, kindness, responsibility, and faith. Students are expected to:

  • Come to class prepared and ready to learn
  • Treat classmates, teachers, and school property with respect
  • Complete assignments on time and to their best ability
  • Participate in prayer and faith formation
  • Ask for help when needed — we are a learning community

🏠 Home – School Partnership

Families are our most important partner in student success. To support learning at home:

  • Read together nightly (20+ minutes)
  • Practice multiplication and division facts
  • Review the week's Essential Question at dinner
  • Check FACTS/RenWeb weekly for grades and communications
  • Email Mrs. Won with questions at any time

📞 Communication

The best way to reach Mrs. Won is by email at ewon@starofthesea.org. Emails will be answered within one school day. Parent-teacher conferences are held quarterly — families may also request additional meetings at any time.
