1. Math Lands First

A math topic that a STEM lab depends on finishes at least one week before the STEM unit begins.

2. Quarterly Sync

A 30-min meeting between Math & STEM at the start of each quarter to confirm prerequisites are taught, not parallel.

3. Co-Graded Labs

A density lab where the math worksheet (units, %-error) is co-graded with the STEM lab report. Math IS the language of science.
