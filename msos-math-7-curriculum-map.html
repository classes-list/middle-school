Beginning of School - Lab Math Refresher CCSS 7.RP.A.3 (Multi-step ratio & percent problems); 7.EE.A.1 (Add/subtract/factor/expand expressions); 7.RP.A.1 (Unit rates with ratios of fractions); 7.NS.A.3 (Real-world problems with rational numbers) 1. Calculate percent error to evaluate measurement accuracy; 2. Use scientific notation with negative exponents for microscopic quantities; 3. Convert between metric units, including rate conversions (km/h ↔ m/s); 4. Distinguish accuracy from precision in lab data. Percent Error, Scientific Notation (positive & negative exponents), Metric Unit Conversions (multi-step rate conversions), Accuracy vs. Precision. Lab: Lab Math Diagnostic — measure, record, and evaluate. Quarter 1: 5 Weeks (8/3-9/4) 1. Diagnostic pre-assessment; 2. Percent error quiz; 3. Scientific notation quiz; 4. Unit conversions quiz; 5. Lab Math Bootcamp report. PhET: Estimation, Number Line: Integers. MSOS Math 7 site: Lab Math units. Khan Academy: 7th grade rates & conversions. Hands-on: lab tools.
Proportional Relationships & Rates CCSS 7.RP.A.1 (Unit rates with ratios of fractions); 7.RP.A.2 (Represent proportional relationships); 7.RP.A.3 (Multi-step ratio & percent problems) 1. Compute unit rates from ratios of fractions; 2. Identify proportional relationships from tables, graphs, equations; 3. Find the constant of proportionality (k = y/x); 4. Solve multi-step ratio, rate, and percent problems. Unit Rates, Constant of Proportionality (k), Proportional Relationships (tables, graphs y = kx, equations), Percent Change, Tax & Tip problems, Distance-Time graphs. Activity: Density-as-rate Lab (g/cm³). Quarter 1: 4 Weeks (9/8-10/2) 1. Unit rates quiz; 2. Proportional relationships quiz; 3. Multi-step percent problems quiz; 4. Density Lab report; 5. Quarter 1 test. PhET: Ratio & Proportion, Graphing Lines. MSOS Math 7 site: Ratios & Proportional Relationships unit. Hands-on: density blocks, balance scales.
Brain Data: Percents, Statistics & Sampling CCSS 7.RP.A.3 (Multi-step percent problems); 7.SP.A.1 (Statistics involves sampling); 7.SP.A.2 (Random sampling for inferences); 7.SP.B.3 (Compare data distributions); 7.SP.B.4 (Measures of center & variability) 1. Calculate percent change (increase/decrease) and percent error in survey data; 2. Distinguish biased from unbiased samples; 3. Design and analyze a representative survey; 4. Compare data distributions using mean, median, MAD. Percent Increase/Decrease, Percent Change in real-world data, Sampling & Bias, Survey Design, Mean Absolute Deviation (MAD), Box Plots, Comparing Data Sets. Activity: Tech & Perception survey — design, run, analyze. Quarter 2: 4 Weeks (10/19-11/13) 1. Percent change quiz; 2. Sampling & bias quiz; 3. Brain Data essay/project; 4. Statistics summary quiz. PhET: Plinko Probability. MSOS Math 7 site: Statistics unit. Survey tools (Google Forms). Hands-on: survey instruments.
Rational Numbers & Algebraic Reasoning CCSS 7.NS.A.1 (Add/subtract rationals); 7.NS.A.2 (Multiply/divide rationals); 7.EE.A.1 (Properties to add, subtract, factor, expand); 7.EE.A.2 (Rewrite expressions); 7.EE.B.3 (Multi-step problems); 7.EE.B.4 (Construct & solve equations); 7.EE.B.4a (Solve px+q=r, p(x+q)=r) 1. Add, subtract, multiply, divide rational numbers (including negatives); 2. Add, subtract, factor, and expand linear expressions; 3. Solve two-step equations; 4. Rearrange formulas to solve for any variable (e.g., D = m/V → m = DV). Rational Number Operations (+, −, ×, ÷ with negatives, fractions, decimals), Linear Expressions (combining like terms, distributive property), Two-Step Equations, Rearranging Formulas (Density, Speed, Area). Activity: Surface-Area-to-Volume ratio investigation. Quarter 2: 5 Weeks (11/16-12/18) 1. Rational ops quiz; 2. Algebraic expressions quiz; 3. Two-step equations quiz; 4. Rearranging formulas quiz; 5. Cell Model project; 6. Quarter 2 test. PhET: Expression Exchange, Equation Grapher. MSOS Math 7 site: Algebraic Reasoning unit. Hands-on: algebra tiles.
Geometry, Inequalities & Probability CCSS 7.G.A.1 (Scale drawings); 7.G.A.2 (Draw shapes with given conditions); 7.G.A.3 (Cross-sections of 3D figures); 7.G.B.4 (Circles: circumference & area); 7.G.B.5 (Angle relationships); 7.G.B.6 (Area, volume, surface area); 7.EE.B.4b (Multi-step inequalities); 7.SP.C.5-8 (Probability) 1. Solve problems with scale drawings; 2. Find area, surface area, volume of triangles, quadrilaterals, prisms, cylinders; 3. Use the circumference and area formulas of circles; 4. Solve multi-step inequalities and graph solutions; 5. Compute theoretical and experimental probabilities of compound events. Scale Drawings, Triangles (constructions, angle sum), Circles (C = 2πr, A = πr²), Area of Composite Figures, Surface Area & Volume of Prisms, Cylinders, Pyramids, Angle Relationships (complementary, supplementary, vertical), Inequalities (multi-step, graphing), Probability (theoretical & experimental, compound events). Quarter 3: 9 Weeks (1/7-3/12) 1. Triangles & angles quiz; 2. Circles quiz; 3. Surface area & volume quiz; 4. Inequalities quiz; 5. Probability quiz; 6. Quarter 3 test. PhET: Area Builder, Reactants Products & Leftovers (ratios), Plinko Probability. MSOS Math 7 site: Geometry & Probability units. Hands-on: protractors, compasses, dice, spinners.
Genetics: Probability, Statistics & Scale CCSS 7.SP.C.7 (Develop probability models); 7.SP.C.8 (Compound events); 7.RP.A.3 (Percent problems); 7.EE.B.3 (Multi-step problems with rational numbers); 7.SP.A.2 (Random sampling); 7.G.A.1 (Scale drawings) 1. Use Punnett squares to predict offspring traits; 2. Model linear vs. exponential population growth; 3. Apply exponential decay to pharmacokinetics (drug half-life); 4. Compute biodiversity indices; 5. Convert actual measurements to scale drawings. Punnett Squares, Compound Probability (independent & dependent events), Population Growth (linear vs. exponential), Drug Half-Life Decay (exponential), Biodiversity Index, Scale Drawings of organisms & habitats. Capstone: Multi-step real-world problems. Quarter 4: 9 Weeks (3/29-6/3) 1. Punnett squares quiz; 2. Population growth project; 3. Pharmacokinetics quiz; 4. Biodiversity project; 5. Year-end capstone test. PhET: Natural Selection, Gene Machine. MSOS Math 7 site: Genetics math unit. Hands-on: paper Punnett squares, M&M genetics activity.
