About Dr. Park

Dr. Park brings a unique blend of global experience, academic rigor, and infectious enthusiasm to every classroom.

Born and raised in South Korea, he moved to Hawai‘i during his sophomore year at Mid-Pacific Institute. He earned a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from Northeastern University in Boston before discovering his true passion for education. He went on to complete a Doctor of Education at Chaminade University of Honolulu.

With 15 years of teaching experience in Catholic schools, Dr. Park has inspired students in math, science, robotics, and engineering.

When not in the classroom, he is an avid motorcycle enthusiast who explores the world on two wheels — you can follow his rides at travelingwoodpecker.com. He also enjoys drawing and watercolor painting; browse a selection of his work in the art gallery. A lifelong student of Taekwondo, he holds a 4th degree black belt. His calmer pursuit is golf, which he credits with keeping him mentally sharp and physically balanced after days with energetic students.
