📐 How we calculated it:
% Error = |measured − actual| ÷ actual × 100%

Percent error measures how far off your measurement is from the real value, as a percentage. Real scientists report percent error to show the precision of their experiments. Under 5% is excellent; under 10% is good; over 25% means you need a better method!
