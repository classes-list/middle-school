Mary, Star of the Sea 7th-grade math is pre-algebra — a course usually reserved for late 7th or 8th grade. We pair the pace with deliberate resequencing so the math arrives right before STEM (science) class needs it. The map below shows the actual quarter-by-quarter outline in our syllabus, with the Glencoe units we draw from, the custom Hawaii- and lab-themed lessons we add, and the science assessment each math unit supports.

Why we resequence Glencoe Pre-Algebra's natural order would put Surface Area : Volume ratio in Unit 4 (spring). Our STEM cell unit starts in January, so we pull SA:V forward to December — two units earlier than the book. Similarly, Punnett-square probability lands by early April for the spring genetics unit. Glencoe chapter tests are still administered.
