Metrics & Scientific Process

The foundation for every science lab this year — how scientists measure, question, and communicate what they find.

🧭 What You'll Learn

This unit is the "how" behind every experiment you'll do in Math + Science this year. You'll master the metric system, learn the tools of the trade, run the scientific method start to finish, practice reporting error, and organize data with real charts.
